How To Read Logs

Introduction

Learn what to look for in the client logs to understand sync progress and common errors.

Initial Startup

Initial startup phase inclues the first ~5 minutes. This phase includes looking for peers and is often filled with warnings / errors until all clients begin syncing.

Syncing

After initial startup, the clients will begin syncing. The CC syncs beacon headers while the EC verifies all past blocks. The intitial sync downloads ~500GB and takes ~24 hours to complete.

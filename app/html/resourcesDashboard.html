Helpful Resources

These resources provide the knowledge and confidence needed to stake from home. Be sure to practice the entire staking process on testnet (Holesky or Sepolia) with test Eth before attempting to run a validator with real Eth on mainnet.

EthStaker Community

EthStaker aims to foster a vibrant and welcoming environment for anyone interested in Ethereum staking. Best way to get help is to post your question in their Discord

EthStaker: Discord | Reddit | Website | FAQ

Somer Esat's Staking Guides

Somer's guides are the 'gold standard' for Ethereum home staking. Vali-Dapp is just an automated version of Somer's guides, so his guides offer a detailed explanation of the setup process for more technical users.
